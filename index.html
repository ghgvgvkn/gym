The moat

The machine map nobody else has.

Every fitness app can generate "a push day." None of them know that your gym's lat pulldown is a plate-loaded unit on the second floor next to the cable crossover. IRONMAP's defensible asset is the per-gym machine graph — real photos, classified against a canonical taxonomy, joined to a biomechanics-grade exercise library. Workouts stop being PDFs and become turn-by-turn navigation of a physical room.
